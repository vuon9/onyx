Our Story

A Passion Born from Precision

ONYX was founded on a simple belief: that a great watch is more than a timekeeper — it is a legacy worn on the wrist, a conversation across generations, a mechanical poem written in gears and springs.

We travel the world to source exceptional timepieces from Switzerland's most revered maisons and independent watchmakers. Every piece in our collection is authenticated, curated, and presented with the respect it deserves.

From the iconic dive watches of Rolex to the grand complications of Patek Philippe, ONYX is your guide to the world of haute horlogerie.
